## PhD and Research Assistant positions

**Research Assistant - Reference Code 7094**
- full-time position (TV-L 13)
- Collaboration in the EXIST research transfer project PuraiteSLR to develop an innovative software solution for automated systematic literature reviews in pharmaceutical and medical research
- Design and implementation of product functions: from requirements analysis and specification to prototyping and user-oriented testing 
- Close collaboration with users (e.g., information specialists, review managers) and integration of feedback into product development 
- Collaboration on software prototypes and support for measurable experiments and an evidence-based development approach
- Responsibility for instrumentation and evaluation of usage data to make data-driven decisions 
- Support in setting up and documenting quality-assured workflows (e.g., data security, traceability, compliance)
- all information here: [Job Announcement Reference Code 7094](https://www.uni-paderborn.de/fileadmin/zv/4-4/stellenangebote/Kennziffer7094.pdf).

### Requirements

Excellent candidates are invited to apply with:

1. Curriculum vitae and copies of degree certificates/transcripts,
2. Writing samples/copies of relevant scientific papers (e.g. thesis),
3. A short statement of research and/or technology interest,
4. Letters of recommendation.
